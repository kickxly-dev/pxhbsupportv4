Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I install P.X HB tools?

Installation is simple! Download the tool from our Discord, run the installer, and follow the on-screen instructions. Most tools install in under 2 minutes.

Is P.X HB safe to use?

Yes! Our tools are built with safety as a top priority. We use advanced detection avoidance and regular updates to ensure maximum security.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Bitcoin, and various cryptocurrencies. All payments are secure and encrypted.

How do I get updates?

Updates are delivered automatically through our launcher. You'll also receive notifications in our Discord server when new versions are available.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our tools, contact support within 7 days for a full refund.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! We provide 24/7 customer support through live chat, Discord, and email. Our average response time is under 2 minutes.
